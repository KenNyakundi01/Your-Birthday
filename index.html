In this web application, the user is able to get the week they were born simply by inserting their birthday and it calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. You will get to know the day you were born depending on the Akan name outputed.

    Male

  1. Sunday: Kwasi
  2. Monday: Kwadwo
  3. Tuesday: Kwabena
  4. Wednesday: Kwaku
  5. Thursday: Yaw
  6. Friday: Kofi
  7. Saturday: Kwame

Female

  1. Sunday: Akosua
  2. Tuesday: Abenaa
  3. Wednesday: Akua
  4. Thursday: Yaa
  5. Friday: Afua
  6. Saturday: Ama
